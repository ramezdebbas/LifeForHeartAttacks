Life for Heart Attack’s:
Description:
Life for Heart Attack’s is an app that provides best and accurate information about Heart Attacks, here you can find the accurate causes of heart attacks, how to test and exams, one of the most important is, which is the best treatment of heart attacks.

Features:
•	Fully medicated research based application on Heart Attacks(also recommended by Doctors)
•	The Research results on the causes of Heart Attacks.
•	The best ways of treatment of Heart Attacks. 
•	Fully Different User Interface Designed keeping in focus point of view of Niche (Target) Market.
•	 Live Tiles(Locally & Remotely)
•	Searching feature.
•	Fully Unique Branding (Unique Logo Designing & Graphics Designing).
